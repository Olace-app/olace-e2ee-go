# Security Policy

This repo is part of the Olace open crypto core. The full security policy, threat model, and disclosure process live in the [Olace transparency repo](https://github.com/Olace-app/transparency/blob/main/SECURITY.md).

Report vulnerabilities to **security@olace.app**. Coordinated disclosure, 90 days. Good-faith research against your own devices and accounts is welcome.
